    Understanding Tax Implications for Large-Cap Investments

    Investing in large-cap funds comes with tax benefits and liabilities:

    • Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) Tax: 10% on gains exceeding â‚¹1 lakh if held for over a year.
    • Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG) Tax: 15% if held for less than a year.
    • Dividends: Dividends are added to taxable income based on the investor's income tax slab.

    Why Investing in Large-Cap Funds Makes Sense

    Large-cap mutual funds focus on industry leaders with a proven history. Benefits include:

    • Resilience in Market Volatility: Large-cap companies maintain stability during market fluctuations due to their robust financials.
    • Reliable Long-Term Returns: Their solid fundamentals lead to consistent growth, benefiting long-term investors.
    • Reduced Risk Exposure: These funds offer security with potential for steady growth.
